9 Tips for Successful Spring Cleaning

Published on March 16th, 2025 By RE/MAX Québec 5 min read

To effectively spring clean your home, you must be organized and methodical. Here's our advice on how to make your spring cleaning successful and efficient.

1. Create a Cleaning Schedule

Start by creating a detailed cleaning schedule. Break down your spring cleaning tasks by room and prioritize them based on importance. This will help you stay organized and ensure nothing gets overlooked.

2. Gather Your Supplies

Before starting, make sure you have all necessary cleaning supplies ready. This includes all-purpose cleaners, microfiber cloths, scrub brushes, vacuum cleaner, and any specific cleaning products for different surfaces.

3. Declutter First

Begin each room by decluttering. Sort items into three piles: keep, donate, and discard. This will make the actual cleaning process much easier and more effective.

4. Top-to-Bottom Approach

Always clean from top to bottom in each room. Start with ceiling fans and light fixtures, then move to walls and windows, and finish with floors. This prevents dust and debris from falling on already cleaned surfaces.

5. Deep Clean Carpets and Upholstery

Spring is the perfect time to deep clean carpets, rugs, and upholstered furniture. Consider professional cleaning services for the best results.

6. Don't Forget the Windows

Clean both sides of windows and window screens. This will let in more natural light and make your home feel brighter and fresher.

7. Address Overlooked Areas

Pay attention to often-overlooked areas like baseboards, door frames, light switches, and cabinet tops. These areas collect dust and grime over time.

8. Organize Storage Spaces

Take time to organize closets, drawers, and storage areas. Use storage solutions to keep items organized and easily accessible.

9. Maintain Regular Cleaning

After your spring cleaning is complete, establish a regular cleaning routine to maintain the results and make next year's spring cleaning easier.
